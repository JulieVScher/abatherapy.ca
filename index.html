Personalized ABA Therapy That Supports Growth and Independence

ABA in the GTA provides compassionate, evidence-based Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA) therapy throughout Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area. Our programs are developed and supervised by Registered Behaviour Analysts (RBA) and delivered by experienced therapists who understand that every child and teen learns differently.

Whether your child or teen is working on communication, social, daily living, or executive functioning skills, our goal is to help them develop confidence and independence through individualized learning experiences that support real-world success.

Services at a Glance

We offer customized ABA programs for children and youth in the Greater Toronto Area. Each program is designed and supervised by a Registered Behaviour Analyst, ensuring quality and ethical practice in every session.

Self-Regulation & Emotional Skills

Helping children and teens identify emotions, manage frustration, and develop coping tools that promote confidence and calm.

Cognitive & Academic Readiness

Building focus, attention, and foundational learning skills to prepare for success in school and daily life.

Social Interaction & Play Skills

Encouraging meaningful connections, turn-taking, and teamwork through structured and natural play opportunities.

Daily Living & Independence

Supporting everyday skills such as self-care, routines, and transitions to promote independence at home and in the community.

About our Founder

Julie Graham, MA, BCBA, R.B.A. (ONT.), CPBAO Registered

Julie is a Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA) and a Registered Behaviour Analyst (RBA) in Ontario, with over a decade of experience supporting children and teens who are neurodiverse through Applied Behaviour Analysis (ABA). She earned her MA in Applied Behaviour Analysis in 2015 and a Specialized Honours BA in Psychology, providing a strong foundation for compassionate, client-centered care. She founded ABA in the GTA Autism Behaviour Therapy, delivering individualized, home-based ABA therapy across the Greater Toronto Area. Through a client-centered approach, she fosters confidence, independence, and meaningful skills across all areas of daily life, tailoring support to each individual’s unique strengths and abilities.
